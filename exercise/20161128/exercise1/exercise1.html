The Chalkwork Family


A hight-quality collection of over 2,500 icons in multiple sizes.


Chalkwork is a visually unified set of carefully designed icons.Built to cover some of the most common icon needs of web and software designers,the entire Chalkwork family offers hunderds of computer and internet-relted metaphors in a visually consistent style at 3 different sizes in up to 6 file formats,as well as alternate vector version in 4 file formts.


Chalkwork is licensed under Creative Commons to be free for personal use,and available for a nominal fee for commerical use.
